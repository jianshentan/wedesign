“Who Is Unknown” is a project that connects innovators and creatives involved in art, design, and technology in a time of digital and social media dependency. “Who Is Unknown” utilizes the physical and digital realm to network, and highlight the community of emerging talents in an un-masked ephemeral physical experience, and in a permanent digital territory. The installation utilizes digital fabrication technologies to create a physical display of faces that appear “unknown,” yet are the faces of emerging talent.
